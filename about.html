About Me

Hello there! My name is Alan and I'm from San Diego, born and raised. In my free time, I like to engage in my various hobbies. I like making art, whether it be digital, painting, or just using a pencil. I specialize in hyper-realistic graphite art, but have been exploring other areas such as digital manga art as well as oil painting. I also enjoy cooking, especially cooking for friends and family. My favorite cuisines to cook are Cantonese comfort food as well as many Japanese dishes. I also like running, rock climbing and going to the gym. My current goal is to run a marathon by the end of summer and gain 10 pounds of muscle by the end of the year. And right now, I'm getting into acoustic guitar. I used to play to violin so it's somewhat similar, but similiar doesn't mean that it's easy. Even though it's harder and I'm only a beginner, I like playing fingerstyle guitar because I find it much more rewarding.

My favorite ice cream flavor is cherry.
